Audi RS7

Description

The Audi RS7 is a blend of luxury and performance, featuring a powerful V8 engine and a sophisticated design.

Images

Interior

Performance

The RS7 boasts a 4.0-liter TFSI twin-turbo V8 engine producing 630 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. It accelerates from 0 to 100 mph in just 3.4 seconds.

Design & Features

The Audi RS7 features a sleek, aggressive exterior with a wide stance and sharp lines. Inside, it offers luxurious materials, advanced technology, and ample space for passengers.

Price

The starting price for the Audi RS7 is around BD55,000 Onwards.
