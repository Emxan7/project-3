BMW M8 Competition

Description

The BMW M8 Competition combines high performance with luxury in a stunning package.

Performance

The BMW M8 Competition is powered by a 4.4-liter BMW M TwinPower Turbo V8 engine that produces 617 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque. It can accelerate from 0 to 100 mph in just 3.2 seconds.

Design & Features

The BMW M8 Competition features a bold and aggressive design with a luxurious interior that includes high-quality materials and advanced technology.

Price

The starting price for the BMW M8 Competition is around BD90,000 Onwards.
