Porsche 911 Turbo S

Description

The Porsche 911 Turbo S is a legendary sports car known for its exceptional performance, luxury, and timeless design.

Images

Interior

Performance

The 911 Turbo S is powered by a 3.8-liter twin-turbo flat-six engine that delivers 640 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. It can accelerate from 0 to 100 mph in just 2.6 seconds.

Design & Features

The Porsche 911 Turbo S features a sleek, aerodynamic design with a luxurious interior that includes advanced technology and premium materials.

Price

The starting price for the Porsche 911 Turbo S is around BD97,000 Onwards.
